Job Description

Immediate need for a talented Video Engineer with experience in the Telecom Industry. This is a 06-12 months contract opportunity with long-term potential and is located in Everett WA . Please review the job description below and contact me ASAP if you are interested.

Job ID. 16-30535

NOTE: Need someone good with Video networks. Candidate should talk about supporting the production network for Video providers as well as working in non-production environments working on engineering projects for upgrades, and improvements. Candidate should be responsible for maintaining Video Services in a Providers Network Operations Center as an engineer working with hard core skills in routing and switching. Client is looking for some at a “Network Professional” level at least. Should have some Content Delivery Network experience.

Summary:


The Senior Video Network Engineer is responsible for engineering IP networks for delivery of triple and quad play video services in Client Network.
This position focuses on the design, evaluation, testing, and implementation of network routing and switching technologies and equipment for the delivery of IP video services to consumers.
The position reports to the Manager of Video Engineering.The person in this position will work with Engineering and Operations teams to evaluate, design, configure, deploy and maintain Video services in the lab and the production network.
This person must have strong troubleshooting skills and will serve as a Tier 3 escalation point for operational issues.

Education/Experience:


BSEE, BSCS, BSET or 8 or more years equivalent experience in a technical/engineering field.
Any of the following technical certifications are strongly desired: Juniper Networks Certified Internet Professional/Expert (JNCIP/JNCIE-M or SP) or equivalent certification from an alternate vendor in a “Service Provider” track (e.g. CCIE).
Cisco Certified Internetwork Expert/Professional (CCIE/CCIP)Skills and Competencies: Strong technical, analytical, and organizational skills associated with the design and implementation of video delivery on IP based networks in service provider environments.
Ability to work in a lab environment, perform hands-on configuration of network equipment, define test plans, and execute tests to meet requirements.
Strong Microsoft Visio, Word, PowerPoint skills.
Presentation and training skills including the ability to clearly present technical material.Major

Job Duties and Responsibilities:

Design and document end-to-end video delivery networks and caching technologies in response to Video system and/or delivery partner requirements.
Author engineering guidelines (EGLs).
Develop test plans and test specific vendor equipment to meet requirements.
Perform lab evaluations on new equipment. Configure routers, switches, servers, streamers, caches, and related systems in a Lab environment for purposes of establishing network-wide implementation procedures and guidelines.
Plan and support the deployment of video and CDN systems across the network.
Train Operations staff on the monitoring and maintenance of the network.
Respond to outages, handle escalations, and assist Operations in troubleshooting.
Handle Tier 3 on-call duties and provide off-hours support for maintenance activities.
Eight or more years of network engineering experience performing hands-on network design, configuration, and troubleshooting of carrier or large enterprise networks, 3+ year experience with video caching, CDN, and video or web acceleration technologies preferred. Telco/Cable experience highly desired.
Strong experience authoring network specifications and diagrams (MS Word/Visio or equivalent)o Strong experience with Juniper MX, M, and/or E series routers (MX960, MX480, MX240, MX80, M320, E320, ERX1440).
Strong experience with JunOS and JunOSe CLI configuration of Juniper subscriber management features for Broadband Network Gateways (BNG) / Broadband Remote Access Servers (BRAS).
Additional experience with high-end Cisco IOS/IOS-XR routers and switches (6509, 7609, ASR, Nexus) desired
Strong experience designing and configuring QoS/CoS for multi-play service provider offerings.
Strong experience with service provider routing and switching protocols and technologies (BGP, OSPF, MPLS/VPLS, VRF/VPN, PIM, STP).
Experience working with multicast and next-generation Video delivery technologies (IPTV, ABR HLS, Smooth Streaming, etc.).
Experience with Web technologies and protocols (HTTP, SSL, HTML, etc.).
Experience with tools for stress testing of networks and related equipment.
Experience with packet/protocol fault analysis for common IP and Ethernet protocols using network traces or sniffer captures. This position is not eligible for OT billing.

Our client is a leading Telecom organization and we are currently interviewing to fill this and other similar contract positions. Qualified candidates should apply NOW for immediate consideration.

If you are interested in this position, please apply online for immediate consideration.
